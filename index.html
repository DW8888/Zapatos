Dubz' Kicks






Humans have always taken pride in presenting their taste, uniqueness, and style. Growing up in the 90s and 2000s, we had a very distinct way of showcasing our tastes: KICKS

There were a vast number of styles, brands, and colors to choose from. As a teen boy who loved basketball, I gravitated toward kicks as not only a coolness measuring stick but also as fertilizer for my dream of growing to be 6'7" and jumping high enough to throw down a windmill. Spoiler alert :that didn't happen.

But every time I slipped into a new pair of kicks, it brought happiness that only makes sense to a 16-year-old kid vying for respect at his local basketball court. So, in the spirit of celebrating youth and style, I'd like to share my favorite kicks of all time!
